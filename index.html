Audio samples for Real-time Speech-to-Speech Dialogue Generation

Abstract: We propose the a real-time speech-to-speech dialogue generation (RTS2S-DG) capable of: (1) generating real-time responses with minimal delay based on streaming input from the conversation. (2) producing coherent and naturalistic responses with spontaneous expressions. (3) enabling fluid turn-taking, including the ability to interrupt or handle interruptions. (4) generating backchannels during others' speech. (5) producing responses of diverse lengths, ranging from short back-and-forth phrases to long-form storytelling formats.
